About Me

I’m a UI/UX Designer with 3+ years of experience designing digital products for both B2B and B2C companies. I’ve worked on SaaS products in the logistics industry and feature request management platforms, where I focused on creating simple, user-friendly, and problem-solving experiences — not just good-looking screens.

I enjoy understanding users through research, competitor analysis, user interviews, and personas, then turning those insights into smooth user flows and interactive UI designs. Along with UI/UX, I also work on branding and creative design, including logos, typography, social media posts, standees, banners, and visual identity systems.

Right now, I’m building a real healthcare project — a doctor appointment booking application — and I’m excited to launch it soon in the real world.

I’m also interested in game development and interior design because I love exploring creativity in different ways. In my free time, I enjoy craft work, making miniatures, and crochet. I also have knowledge in fashion design, from fashion illustration and stitching garments to T-shirt printing using both manual and machine methods.

I believe teaching others is one of the best ways to keep learning, and that mindset always motivates me to grow and improve as a designer and creator.
